# 💄 Cosmetic Chemical Awareness Project

## 📌 Project Overview
[span_0](start_span)This project investigates the presence and frequency of harmful chemicals (such as parabens, phthalates, and formaldehyde) in various cosmetic products[span_0](end_span). [span_1](start_span)Using data analysis and machine learning, the study identifies hazardous formulations to enhance consumer awareness and support safer product choices[span_1](end_span).

---

## 🛠 Methodology & Technical Implementation
* **[span_2](start_span)Data Acquisition:** Gathered ingredient lists from cosmetic databases and regulatory sources[span_2](end_span).
* **[span_3](start_span)Data Preparation:** Standardized ingredient names and engineered a target feature `is_harmful` based on known hazardous substances[span_3](end_span).
* **[span_4](start_span)Machine Learning:** Developed a **Logistic Regression** model to predict the presence of harmful chemicals using vectorized ingredient text (CountVectorizer)[span_4](end_span).
* **[span_5](start_span)Analytics:** Conducted statistical grouping by brand and category to identify high-risk product groups[span_5](end_span).

---

## 📊 Key Visualizations
The project includes several analytical charts:
* **[span_6](start_span)Risk Distribution:** Proportion of harmful vs. non-harmful ingredients[span_6](end_span).
* **[span_7](start_span)Brand & Category Analysis:** Identification of top 10 categories and brands associated with hazardous chemicals[span_7](end_span).
* **[span_8](start_span)Predictive Performance:** Evaluation through confusion matrices and classification reports[span_8](end_span).
